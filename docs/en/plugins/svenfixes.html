svenfixes

Fix multiple errors and/or problems that exists in Sven Co-op and are possible to fix with AngelScript.

Fixes the next problems:

ScriptDescriptionCredits
observer_noisesPlayers on observer mode normally does water noises by passing through certain surfaces.Rick
grenade_revivableNormally grenades can be revived, the players exploits this because when a grenade is revived it does generate Z velocity sending the players to the sky.
observer_presenceSpectators can be seen and greeted by npcs with flag "Wait til seen" breaking some maps.Rick
gravity_lostWhen the gravity of an individual player is modified and then this player die, when revived he will lost it's gravity, if that is necesary for a jump, this player won't be able to keep going.
satchel_stun"Inteligent" Npcs that detects explosions are "Stunned" when throwing a c4 around them but not exploding, players exploits this bug and let enemies unable to move.Giegue
deadplayer_sinkPlayer corpses can sink into the ground if certain space conditions are met.CubeMath
tripmine_spamWhen you are on a map that spawns tripmines in a short period, players are able to crash the server by placing a big ammount of tripmines on the same spot.
gonome_crouchWhen you are on the melee range of a gonome, if you press control (duck) repeated times, this won't be able to attack you.
hwgrunt_crouchWhen you are on the melee range of a grunt with minigun, if you hold control (duck) it's bullets won't hit you.
longjump_reviveWhen you die having the long jump module and you are revived you will lose this item.Kezaeiv

Requires metamod.
